## Copy bit to bit 
dd if=inputfile of=outputfile
>[!example]
>kali >dd if=/dev/sdb of=/root/flashcopy
1257441=0 records in
1257440+0 records out
7643809280 bytes (7.6 GB) copied, 1220.729 s, 5.2 MB/s
